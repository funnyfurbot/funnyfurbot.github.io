FunnyFurBot

FunnyFurBot is a local desktop application that helps automate the upload of funny pet videos to your personal YouTube channel.

  • It uses the YouTube Data API to post short videos (Shorts) with titles and descriptions generated by AI (ChatGPT).
  • No data is collected, stored, or transmitted from users.
  • The app runs only on your own device and uploads content only to your own authenticated YouTube account.

This tool is designed for personal use only, to streamline content creation for animal-themed YouTube Shorts.
