Privacy Policy

This app, FunnyFurBot, does not collect, store, or share any user data.

All data handled via this app remains on the user’s local device and is used only to publish content on their own YouTube channel via the YouTube Data API.

We do not transmit any information to external servers. OAuth login is required solely to allow video uploads to the user's YouTube account.
